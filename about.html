About Tutlee

Tutlee is an AI-powered tutoring platform that helps students learn smarter, not harder — through adaptive sessions, real-time feedback, and a community of learners.

Our Mission

We believe every student deserves access to personalised, high-quality education. Tutlee combines cutting-edge AI with expert-designed curricula to create learning experiences that adapt to each individual learner.

Our Story

Tutlee was founded by a team of educators and engineers who saw a gap between what students needed and what traditional tutoring could provide. We set out to build a platform that scales the best of human teaching through technology.

Our Team

We are a small, passionate team spread across the globe, united by the belief that learning is one of the most important things a person can do. We are always looking for talented people who share our mission.
