Safety at Tutlee

We are committed to providing a safe, respectful, and inclusive learning environment for all users.

Community Standards

All users of Tutlee are expected to treat each other with respect. Harassment, bullying, hate speech, and any form of discrimination are strictly prohibited and may result in immediate account termination.

Content Safety

Tutlee uses automated and human moderation to ensure that all content on the platform is appropriate and accurate. If you encounter content that you believe violates our standards, please report it immediately.

Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your personal data. We never sell your data to third parties. For more details, please see our Privacy Policy.

Reporting Concerns

If you have a safety concern or need to report an incident, contact us at safety@tutlee.com. We take all reports seriously and will respond promptly.
