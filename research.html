Research

At Tutlee we invest in learning science research to ensure our platform is grounded in evidence-based pedagogy.

Our Approach

We collaborate with educators, cognitive scientists, and learning researchers to continuously improve how Tutlee teaches. Our adaptive algorithms are informed by decades of research into spaced repetition, retrieval practice, and metacognitive learning strategies.

Key Research Areas

Adaptive Learning Systems

How AI can personalise content delivery to match each learner's current knowledge state and learning pace.

Spaced Repetition

Optimising review schedules so students retain information for the long term, not just for the next test.

Formative Assessment

Using frequent, low-stakes assessments to guide instruction and surface misconceptions early.
